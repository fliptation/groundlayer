# Ground Layer

**An open-source blueprint for a healthier planet and people.**

Less control and force. More unity in people. Thirteen interconnected layers covering how we grow food, care for health, govern ourselves, exchange value, learn together, power our lives, shelter each other, build tools, protect water, move freely, share stories, keep each other safe, and defend human rights.

## The Thirteen Layers

1. **[Food & Land](docs/food-and-land.md)** — Community land trusts, open-source agriculture, local food networks
2. **[Health](docs/health.md)** — Prevention first, open medical knowledge, community health circles
3. **[Governance](docs/governance.md)** — Subsidiarity, liquid democracy, rotating stewardship
4. **[Economy](docs/economy.md)** — Commons-based production, local currencies, time banking
5. **[Education & Culture](docs/education.md)** — Learning by doing, open knowledge, skills that matter
6. **[Energy & Environment](docs/energy.md)** — Distributed renewables, rights of nature, circular materials
7. **[Housing & Shelter](docs/housing.md)** — Co-housing, community land trusts, open-source architecture
8. **[Technology & Infrastructure](docs/technology.md)** — Open-source tools, mesh networks, data sovereignty
9. **[Water & Sanitation](docs/water.md)** — Community water stewardship, watershed protection, sanitation as a right
10. **[Transportation & Mobility](docs/transportation.md)** — Walkable communities, transit cooperatives, shared mobility
11. **[Communication & Media](docs/communication.md)** — Community media, decentralized platforms, media literacy
12. **[Safety & Conflict Resolution](docs/safety.md)** — Community safety networks, restorative justice, mutual aid
13. **[Human Rights](docs/human-rights.md)** — Citizen documentation, open data, rights education, decentralized accountability

## Core Principles

- **Decentralize power** — no single entity controls what everyone depends on
- **Transparency by default** — trust through visibility, not authority
- **Regeneration over extraction** — give back more than you take
- **Consent over coercion** — participation is voluntary
